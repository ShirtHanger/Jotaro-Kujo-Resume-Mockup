My Story

From a young age, I have been fascinated by the ocean and its mysteries.

This passion led me to pursue a career in marine science, where I have dedicated myself to understanding and protecting marine ecosystems.

My journey has taken me from my home in the shores of Japan to the research labs of the United States, where I have honed my skills and expanded my knowledge.

My Proud Lineage

As a descendant of the esteemed Joestar family, I carry a legacy of courage, integrity, and a relentless pursuit of justice. These values have shaped my approach to both my personal and professional life, driving me to make meaningful contributions to the field of marine biology.

My Work

Over the years, I have had the privilege of working on various research projects that focus on marine biodiversity, conservation, and the impacts of climate change on ocean ecosystems.

My work has been published in several scientific journals, and I have presented my findings at international conferences. I am particularly proud of my contributions to coral reef restoration efforts, which aim to preserve these vital habitats for future generations.
